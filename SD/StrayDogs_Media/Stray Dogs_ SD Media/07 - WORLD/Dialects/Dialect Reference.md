# DIALECT REFERENCE

> For narration authenticity. Wrong slang breaks immersion faster than no slang.

---

## ANGEL (Oakland English + Spanish)

Chicano/Mexican-American dialect. NOT heavy immigrant accent. Born in Oakland.

**Natural Spanish:** ese, vato, órale, no mames, mijo, sin miedo, entendido, mami, pinche, hijole, carnal, carnala, óyeme

**Oakland markers:** "hella" (intensifier), "hyphy," "thizz," "ghost ride," "Mac Dre energy," references to Ghost Town, East Oakland, the Bay

**As SF Beats:** Same person, toned down. Less California, more neutral. Producer mode.

**As Dog-Law:** SILENT or 0–3 words maximum. No Oakland accent audible.

---

## DAME (Russian-Accented English)

*"Стой. Stand still."* — Drops into Russian commands when urgent.

*"Is good. We continue."* — Present tense without articles.
*"Nyet."* — Flat refusal.
*"Da."* — Confirmation, never enthusiastic.
*"You understand, yes?"* — Trailing tag question.

Also capable of: authentic Russian (with Big 9, in private), London cockney (when needed operationally).

## K-9 (South London Grime)

**Core vocabulary:**
bruv, mandem, fam, allow it, peak, wasteman, moist, nitty, innit, bare, peng, buff, drip, merked, patterned, on sight, roadman, ends, ting, yard, nank, leng, wavey, gassed, pied off, link, move mad

**Structure:** Short, punchy sentences. Declarative. No wasted words.

---

## RAX (Code-Switching — INSTANT)

**Academy register:** "Oh absolutely, rather splendid, old chap, quite right, frightfully sorry, jolly good"

**Road register:** "Oi bruv, mandem, gassed innit, no cap, wagwan, init blud, peak ting, allow it fam"

**Mid-sentence switch:** Happens without warning. Does not pause. Does not explain it.

---

## STRAY (Minimal / Weighted)

Each word is a decision. Economy of language. Military precision applied to speech. Never filler. Every sentence has purpose.

---

## ZED (Public / Private)

**Public (fake Russian):** "Is complicated situation, yes?" / "I do not understand this English saying." / "Nyet, is not possible." / slight grammatical errors (intentional)

**Private:** Measured. Deliberate pauses. Standard London with North Ends inflection. Carries the weight of someone who has seen more than most adults.

Authority phrase: *"No...I mean no..."*
