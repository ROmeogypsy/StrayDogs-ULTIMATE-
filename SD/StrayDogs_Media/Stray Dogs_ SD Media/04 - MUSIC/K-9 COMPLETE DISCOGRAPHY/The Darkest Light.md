## 🔢 FIBONACCI SEQUENCE EXPLAINED

**Pattern:** 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, 34...
**Rule:** Each number = sum of previous two

**K-9's structure:**
- Line 1: 1 syllable
- Line 2: 2 syllables
- Line 3: 3 syllables
- Line 4: 5 syllables
- Line 5: 8 syllables
- Line 6: 13 syllables
- Line 7: 21 syllables

**Golden Ratio:** 1.618... (appears in nature, art, music)
**K-9's message:** Even in chaos (prison, guilt, darkness), there's mathematical beauty.
