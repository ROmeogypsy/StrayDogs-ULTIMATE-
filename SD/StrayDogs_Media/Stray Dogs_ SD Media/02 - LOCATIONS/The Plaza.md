# THE PLAZA

> The geographic threshold where the grit of the North Ends meets the polish of the West End.

---

## WHAT IT IS

A 24-hour neutral zone. Fuelled by:
- A central coffee shop (the only true DMZ in the area)
- **The Fountain** — central landmark, Z-Artist's chalk mural canvas, site of the Fountain Incident
- Constant friction between navy blazers and grey juvie jumpsuits

**Jurisdiction:** Monitored by St. Augustine Prefects. Police only enter if summoned by the Academy.

---

## THE FOUNTAIN

Zed creates elaborate chalk murals here. Most recent: a snarling dog with **66 tally marks** — one for each Dog. The murals wash away in rain — intentionally. Art as impermanence.

K-9 flung a lacrosse ball toward the fountain. Rax lunged. Alistair watching. 30 NEPH Dogs lounging. Zed finishing a chalk mural. The blazers and jumpsuits nearly collided. K-9 and Rax defused it through public embarrassment.
