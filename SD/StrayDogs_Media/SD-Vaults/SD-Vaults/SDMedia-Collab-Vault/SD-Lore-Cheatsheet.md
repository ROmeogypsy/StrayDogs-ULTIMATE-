## The Theater: The Plaza
- 24-hour neutral zone. Central coffee shop.
- Where The Hill (Navy Blazers) and NEPH (Grey Juvie Jumpsuits) cohabitate uneasily.
- Monitored by St. Augustine Prefects. Police only enter if summoned.
- **Lyrical significance:** The Plaza is the stage. Every SD Media track lives here at some level.

## Key Characters (Songwriting Lens)

### K-9 (Kian, 16) — "The Voice"
- Big 9's younger brother. Famous YouTube rapper.
- Rax's best friend. SD Media's aggressive energy.
- **Write him as:** fire that doesn't know it's burning itself.

### Big 9 — "The Founder"
- Incarcerated at Wormwood Scrubs. Whole Life Order.
- Dame's fiancé. Took the fall to protect K-9.
- **Write him as:** the absence that shapes everything else.

### Dame — "The Matriarch"
- Producer/Videographer. Runs The Kennel.
- Manages Zed's "Russian" cover. Controls branding.
- **Write her as:** the calm at the center of the storm.

### Zed Kidd (17) / Z-Artist — "The Zombie"
- Wanted dropout. Stays masked. Fake Russian accent to outsiders.
- Rax's identical twin. Scarred (truck + 7 stabs).
- **Write him as:** art made out of surviving what should have ended him.

### Rax Kidd (17) — "The Heart"
- St. Augustine star athlete. Unscarred. Identical to Zed.
- Goofy until he goes "Teeth Down."
- **Write him as:** the joke that stops being funny.

### Stray — "The Ghost"
- Moves like he doesn't belong to any postcode.
- **Write him as:** the witness. The one who saw everything and never said.

### Dog-Prophet — "The Word"
- The conscience of SD Media.
- **Write him as:** every consequence given a voice before it arrives.

### Chew-toy (18) — "The Anchor"
- 6'3" NEPH senior. Dame's successor. Manages the 30 Dogs.
- Covers for Zed at school ("He's in the back of Maths").

### Snout — "The Vet"
- Zed's mentor. Community liaison. Federal tracker.
- Created and enforces the "Silence" protocol.
