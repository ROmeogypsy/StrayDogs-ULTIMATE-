# Z-Artist — Writer Profile
**Real Name:** Zed Kidd
**Age:** 17
**Tag:** "The Zombie" / "Z-Artist"

---

## 🎤 Voice & Style
- **Energy:** Cold, precise, controlled detonation. Violence wrapped in art.
- **Flow Tendencies:** Methodical bars, long builds, imagery-heavy. Murals in audio form.
- **Themes:** Duality (Zed vs. Rax), the mask as identity, survival through transformation, the scarred body as text
- **Dialect:** The "Russian" persona bleeds into the art — deliberate dislocation, foreignness as weapon
- **Signature Move:** The mural bar — a full 8-bar sequence that functions like a single visual image
- **Note:** Lyrics often feel like they're written *on* something. Chalk. Skin. Concrete.

---

## 🔗 Relationships (for lyrical mining)
- **Rax** — Identical twin. The unscarred version of himself. Every Zed bar contains a Rax ghost.
- **Snout** — Mentor. The silence Zed learned from.
- **Dame** — She manages his cover. He trusts her with his face. That's everything.
- **K-9** — Admires the fire. Knows he doesn't have it. Writes the cold so K-9 can bring the heat.
