# Dog-Prophet Commandments
**Tags:** #lore #dog-prophet #commandments #reference

> The operating laws of Dog-Prophet's lyrical world. These are not rules for writing — they are the worldview embedded in every bar.

---

## The Commandments

1. **What is spoken becomes law.** The bar is the contract.

2. **Every debt gets collected.** The street doesn't forget — it just waits.

3. **The silent one saw everything.** (For Stray. Always for Stray.)

4. **A name carries its holder's weight.** Say "Big 9" and you carry everything he did.

5. **Sacrifice is not loss — it is investment.** Big 9's sentence is interest-bearing.

6. **The mask is not deception. The mask is mercy.** (For Z-Artist. The face they don't see can't be targeted.)

7. **Fire burns everything including its source.** (For K-9. Always.)

8. **The Kennel is not a location. It is a condition.**

9. **You are not where you are from. You are what you returned to.**

10. **The prophecy is not a warning. It is a memory of what hasn't happened yet.**
