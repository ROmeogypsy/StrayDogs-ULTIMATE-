## INITIATION PROTOCOL

1. **Nomination** — Existing Dog vouches (Stray vouched for Scraps, Dame vouched for Angel)
2. **Vetting** — High Command vote (unanimous for acceptance)
3. **Red insignia tattoo** — Auntie Kat marks them (location varies, always visible)
4. **Burning of old identity** — Scraps burning Academy ID (current), Angel burned Oakland passport (Track 04)
5. **First shift** — Immediate assignment to role/crew
6. **Public announcement** — Told to all 66 at Kennel meeting
