## POLICE RELATIONSHIP

**Metropolitan Police aware** — tolerate it because:
1. Violence in district dropped 80% since Dogs took over
2. Workers more willing to report serious crimes (trust the Dogs more than police)
3. Dogs don't deal drugs, don't traffic, don't pimp (strict code)
4. Arresting the Dogs would create a power vacuum (worse alternatives would fill it)

**Unspoken agreement:** Dogs keep it quiet, police look the other way. If it gets loud (media attention, deaths, trafficking), the truce ends.
