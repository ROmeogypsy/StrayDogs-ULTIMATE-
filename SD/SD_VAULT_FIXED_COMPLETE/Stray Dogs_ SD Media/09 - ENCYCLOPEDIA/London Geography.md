# LONDON GEOGRAPHY — SD MEDIA UNIVERSE

## NORTH ENDS (Primary Territory)

**Character:** Working-class, diverse, high-density housing estates, street culture, grime music origin point

**Boundaries:**
- North: Ends at industrial canal
- South: [[The Plaza]] (neutral zone before West End)
- East: Marlowe Street → [[The Kennel]] (47 Marlowe Street Basement)
- West: NEPH school territory

**Key Locations:**
- [[The Kennel]] — 47 Marlowe Street Basement (SD Media HQ)
- [[Red Light District]] — 17 buildings under SD protection
- [[The Hydrant]] — Municipal pipe, protection base
- [[The Plaza]] — Neutral zone, Zed's chalk mural canvas
- [[TIMTIMS Rooftop]] — Where Scraps was accepted (65th Dog)
- North Ends Tunnel — Under Marlowe Street embankment (Track 04 filmed here)

**Demographics:**
- 60% council housing (government-subsidized)
- 40% working poor, immigrants, students
- Street market Saturdays (Zed sells artwork here)
- Grime music culture strong

**Police Presence:**
- Metropolitan Police patrol 2x daily
- Generally avoid Red Light (unspoken agreement with Dogs)
- Will respond to Academy calls (St. Augustine's influence)

---

## WEST END (Rival Territory)

**Character:** Gentrified, expensive, "wannabe posh" (K-9's words), gang operates in designer labels

**Key Gang:** West End Crew (Scraps' former affiliation)

**Tension:** Scraps' betrayal + Shaw coat destruction = active retaliation threat

---

## THE HILL (St. Augustine's Prep Territory)

**Character:** Old money, private school grounds, gates, navy blazers, lacrosse fields

**Embedded Dogs:** K-9, Rax, 3 others (intelligence operation)

**Key Figures:**
- Alistair Delarusso (Head Boy)
- Nala Regan (Head Girl — investigating SD Media)
- Prefect Leo (building expulsion files)

**Geography:**
- Gates visible from [[The Plaza]] fountain
- Lacrosse fields north side
- Main building = Gothic Revival architecture
- Prefects patrol grounds

---

## SOUTH ENDS PUBLIC (SEPH) — "The Yard"

**Character:** The middle ground — not as rough as NEPH, not as posh as The Hill

**Relationship to Dogs:** Neutral, some students buy SD Media merch

**Character:** Rough, 30 Dogs attend here, grey jumpsuits resemble prison uniforms (intentional aesthetic)

## TRANSIT

**The Tube:** Northern Line runs through North Ends (Angel uses this to reach Heathrow initially)

**Buses:** 24-hour service (Dogs use these for late-night shifts)

**Thames:** Southern boundary of Red Light District (escape route if needed)

## WEATHER & ATMOSPHERE

**Winter (Current — February):** 3-8°C, frequent rain, grey skies, early darkness (4:30 PM sunset)

**Signature sound:** Rain on concrete, double-decker buses, sirens distant, grime bass through estate windows

**Signature smell:** Wet pavement, kebab shops, diesel, cigarette smoke, Thames river (damp, industrial)
