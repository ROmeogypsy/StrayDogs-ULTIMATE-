SFO airport tarmac at sunset — neon pink/purple/orange sky. [[Angel Reyes]] shirtless, vitiligo visible, GT neck tattoo, Dmitri's chain, blue bandana back pocket, khaki Dickies, Nike Cortez. Oakland skyline distant, departing planes. Direct eye contact.
