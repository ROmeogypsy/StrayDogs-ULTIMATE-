## 🔄 BACKWARDS TEXT DECODED

**"Nopaew ruoy Tesr"** → Reversed: **"Rest your paweN"**

### What It Means
- **"Rest your paweN"** = Put down your weapon (paw = dog, paweN = weapon play on words)
- **NEPH tribute** = North Ends Public High kids who taught rhetoric over violence
- **Cipher element** = First time Angel used Zed's backwards text technique publicly

### Why Backwards
Angel learned this from Zed post-arrival in London. This track shows:
1. He absorbed Zed's technique
2. He's honoring NEPH kids (30 Dogs from that school)
3. He's establishing cipher as SD Media standard

## 💡 THEME BREAKDOWN

### Rhetoric vs. Repentance
| Rhetoric | Repentance |
|----------|------------|
| Aggressive speech | Humble acknowledgment |
| "6s and 7s" (dice/gambling metaphor) | Genuine change |
| Condescendance | Learning from "next kin" |
| Weapon-based threat | Understanding-based peace |

**Angel's message:** NEPH kids taught him that real power is rhetoric (skilled speech) delivered with repentance (humility), not condescension.

### "6s and 7s"
- British slang: "at sixes and sevens" = confused, disorganized
- Angel: "it's not all violence and chaos"
- Acknowledges messiness without glorifying it

### "Expect tin"
- **Tin** = worthless/cheap (British slang)
- When people don't understand, they expect nothing (tin)
- NEPH taught Angel to give gold (rhetoric) not tin (empty threats)
