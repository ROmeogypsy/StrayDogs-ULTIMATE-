## PHYSICAL

Always masked in public. Chalk-stained fingers. At mask edges: facial asymmetry, embedded asphalt fragments in skin, jaw misalignment, one eye reflects light wrong. Heavy fake Russian accent for any outsider.

**The scars** came from late 2023: dragged 3 blocks behind a truck (driven by [[Scraps Harper]], then called Harper), also stabbed 7 times. Rivals saw a power vacuum after Big 9's arrest. Zed survived. The damage did not. Scars are permanent.

---

## THE ARTIST

As Z-Artist, creates elaborate chalk murals at [[The Plaza]] fountain. Geometric patterns, flowing lines, aggressive beauty. Most recent: snarling dog with **66 tally marks** (one per Dog). The murals wash away in rain — intentionally. Art as impermanence. Zed is the only Dog who makes something beautiful knowing it will be destroyed.

---

## COMBAT

The drag removed his limits. Most dangerous Dog in direct confrontation. Lethal without hesitation.

---

## TWIN CONNECTION

[[Rax Kidd]] FEELS Zed's emotional state — not telepathy, instinctual bond. When Zed was dragged, Rax woke up screaming. Zed's standing order to the Dogs: *"Don't let Rax see"* his worst moments.

## VOICE (private)

When not performing the Russian cover: measured, economical, deliberate pauses. Authority phrase: *"No...I mean no..."* Carries the weight of someone who has seen more than most adults.
