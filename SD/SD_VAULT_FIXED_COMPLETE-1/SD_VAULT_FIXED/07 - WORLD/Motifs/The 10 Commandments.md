# THE 10 COMMANDMENTS (DOG-SCRIPTURE)

Painted on the North Ends tunnel wall in [[Track 04 — Dog-Scripture]]. Also painted on the back of Dog-Prophet's jumpsuit. Also on the wall of [[The Kennel]].

---

```
1. THOU SHALT NOT SNITCH
2. HONOR THE DEAD
3. FEAR NO EVIL (SIN MIEDO)
4. PROTECT THE SET
5. TRUST NO BITCH
6. KEEP THE STEEL COLD
7. WATCH THE THRONE
8. NEVER FORGET HOME
9. SILENCE IS GOLD
10. DOG EATS DOG
```

**Closing prayer:** *"Dog our Martyr in greezey we play..... Amen"*

---

## WHAT TRACK 09 DID TO THESE

| Commandment | Status after Track 09 |
|-------------|----------------------|
| #1 THOU SHALT NOT SNITCH | Angel confessed publicly. He broke it — or redefined it. |
| #2 HONOR THE DEAD | The entire album honors the dead. This one held. |
| #3 FEAR NO EVIL (SIN MIEDO) | Track 09 shows he feared everything. He said it anyway. |
| #6 KEEP THE STEEL COLD | Murder confession. Steel was not cold. |
| #9 SILENCE IS GOLD | He broke his silence entirely. |

Track 09 is not a violation of the commandments. It is what happens when someone takes Commandment #2 seriously enough to risk everything else.
