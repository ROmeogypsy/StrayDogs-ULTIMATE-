# SYLLABIC EMBEDDING

Angel's signature technique. Creating **multiple semantic layers within single phonetic sequences**. Every bar carrying multiple simultaneous readings.

The listener hears one thing. A second reading exists. Sometimes a third.

---

## EXAMPLES

| Line | Surface Reading | Embedded Reading |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| "Wine dykes → Whines rights" | Wine enthusiasts | LGBTQ+ rights (K-9's bisexuality coded) |
| "H is in ome ijo" | Letter H is in "home" | Spanish: *hijo* (son) — H is in homie AND hijo |
| "bucks back the dear is gone" | Money left when deer fled | Money gone / dear person gone / Dmitri gone |
| "Do ne rro fo flo" | Nonsense syllables | Reversed: "Dinero for flow" — *dinero* = money |

---

## THE PRINCIPLE

Sound and meaning are independent systems. A syllable can mean one thing phonetically and another thing semantically. Angel exploits the gap between how words sound and what they spell, between what a listener hears and what a reader sees.

Each bar is a puzzle that rewards re-listening.
