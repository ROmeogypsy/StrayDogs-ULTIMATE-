## writer profile

Stray writes from the margins. Observer. Witness. What's *not* said lands harder than what is.

Flow: The Stray Drift — meanders, then lands. Pauses are intentional. Bars sit behind the beat.

The Pivot is everything. A bar that seems to be about one thing, lands on something completely different.
