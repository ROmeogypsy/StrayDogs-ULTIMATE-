# Stray — Writer Profile
**Tag:** "The Ghost" / The Drifter
**Status:** Active — SD Media

---

## 🎤 Voice & Style
- **Energy:** Elusive, atmospheric, speaks from the margins
- **Flow Tendencies:** Slower deliberate cadence, spaces between bars matter as much as the words — what's *not* said
- **Themes:** Displacement, survival without a fixed identity, the cost of freedom, the street as a kind of exile
- **Dialect:** Fluid — drifts between registers. No fixed postcode in the voice either.
- **Signature Move:** The pivot — a bar that seems to be about one thing, lands on something completely different

---

## 🔗 Relationships (for lyrical mining)
- **The Mandem** — Part of it, but never quite tethered. The Dog who moves like a stray.
- **The Plaza** — Stray's relationship to the space is different. Observer. Witness.
- **K-9** — Fire vs. smoke. They complement, rarely collab, but when they do it hits different.
- **Z-Artist** — Shared silence. Two people who communicate in what they don't say.
