# SYMBOLS & RECURRING MOTIFS

---

## MUZZLE UP / TEETH DOWN
Core Dogs philosophy. Controlled and strategic vs. violence unleashed. The line between them is the Dogs' power. They choose when to bite.

## SIN MIEDO
*"Without Fear"* — Reyes family creed. Commandment #3 on the tunnel wall. Spoken by Lux on Track 06. Becomes the entire organisation's mantra.

| Track | Appearance |
|-------|------------|
| Track 01 | Introduced (first bar) |
| Track 04 | Commandment #3 — FEAR NO EVIL (SIN MIEDO) |
| Track 06 | Lux says it on the phone call |
| Track 09 | Fear breaks through anyway |

## THE CHAIN
Dmitri's silver chain. On Angel's neck — NEVER REMOVED. Physical tether to a dead man. Sacred object. Appears in every video, every scene, every description.

## THE LOGGING CHAIN
Zed's logging chain — the object he was dragged with. Trauma made object. Metaphorical: connection, loyalty, bondage. In "The Dialogue" skit, K-9 tells Zed to move it. Zed says: *"No...I mean no."*

## RED TINGS
Guns, violence, blood — but also the red markings on Dogs. Red paint on Dog-Law's blue jumpsuit. Red paw on the ballistic masks. Dame's tattoo ink. Everything red in this universe has cost something.

## BACKWARDS TEXT (ZED'S CIPHER)
Zed hides messages in reversed text in his songs. Community members who catch it understand. Examples:
- `RETTEB EB TEL EB` = **BE LET BE BETTER**
- `Nopaew ruoy Tesr` = **Rest your weapon**

## THE ASTERISK (\*)
`at-risks (*)` = at-risk youth + asterisk notation. "Grime rap straight from the \*" — K-9's signature adopted across SD Media.

## BIG 9 → K-9 → STRAY
Father → Son → Holy Ghost. The lineage of SD Media's true power. Big 9 is the founder. K-9 is the public voice. Stray is the voice no one has heard — the nuclear one.

## THE COAT
Captain Shaw's HM Royal Guard jacket. Stray's most sacred possession. Given to Scraps. Scraps' crew shredded it. The loss is permanent. The meaning is not.

## TARGET
Rax's nickname for Angel. He deliberately puts his face, Oakland tattoos, and dead family's photographs on a global album release to draw every police force and rival gang's attention onto himself — to protect the other 66 Dogs.

## RAIN
London rain. Track 01. Track 10. The alley where Stray found Scraps. The Plaza in winter. Rain = London = grief = cleansing. Never stops.

## FIRE / BURNING
Track 04: Burning passport and files. Track 08: *"I burned it all down to flush the demons out."* Purging. Irreversible choices. You cannot unburn.

## THE FOUNTAIN
The Plaza fountain. Z-Artist's canvas. Where the chalk murals live and die. Where Angel and K-9 sat on opposite sides and found they were on the same fountain. Where impermanence is the point.
