# SIN MIEDO

*"Without Fear"* — Spanish. The Reyes family creed.

## MEANING

[[Lux Reyes]] raised Angel with this creed. It was her armour for a son she was training to survive in a world that would try to kill him. She believed it hard enough to pass it to [[Dmitri Volkov]], who believed it hard enough to raise Angel like it was his own inheritance.

It became Commandment #3 because [[Angel Reyes]] made it institutional. He took his mother's creed and wrote it into stone on a tunnel wall in North Ends, London.

Track 09 shows that it was never really *without* fear. It was *despite* fear. That's a different thing.
