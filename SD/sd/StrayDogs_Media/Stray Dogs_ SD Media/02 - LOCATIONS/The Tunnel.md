# NORTH ENDS TUNNEL

> The tunnel runs under the Marlowe Street rail embankment. Damp concrete. The kind of dark that has texture.

---

## WHAT IS HERE

The 10 Commandments — painted on the curved concrete wall in letters 60 centimetres tall:

1. THOU SHALT NOT SNITCH
2. HONOR THE DEAD
3. **FEAR NO EVIL (SIN MIEDO)**
4. PROTECT THE SET
5. TRUST NO BITCH
6. KEEP THE STEEL COLD
7. WATCH THE THRONE
8. NEVER FORGET HOME
9. SILENCE IS GOLD
10. DOG EATS DOG

*Dog our Martyr in greezey we play..... Amen*

---

## THE RITUAL (Track 04)

16 masked Dogs. Blue jumpsuits. Ballistic masks with red paw prints. Files and passport burned in a steel bucket. Unison Amen echoing off curved walls.

**The Dog-Scripture video was filmed here. It is vaulted.** Dame has it on a hard drive only she can access.
