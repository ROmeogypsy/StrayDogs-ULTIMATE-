## THE NIGHT STRAY FOUND HIM

Alley off Third. Raining. Scraps had nowhere to be and wasn't planning on going anywhere. Stray sat with him all night without speaking. In the morning: phone charger, [[Kennel]] access, **Captain Shaw's Royal Guard jacket** — [[Stray]]'s most sacred possession, the last thing he had of his father.

Harper's crew shredded the coat when they found him wearing it.

Harper warned Stray. Fought his own crew 4v1. Saved Stray.

[[Zed]] accepted him on [[TIMTIMS Rooftop]]: *"Harper's dead. You're Scraps now."*
