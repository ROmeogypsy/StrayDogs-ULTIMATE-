## NARRATIVE FUNCTION

The apex. This is the highest point of the album before the fall. Track 07 → Track 08 is the vision of what success could feel like → Track 09 is the price. *"The world knows my name"* said from the Plaza fountain, navy-blazered St. Augustine's students watching through the gate bars. They don't know what they're looking at.
