## INTERVIEW PART 1

**Dog Profit:** You said the dog just... sat with you.
**Scraps:** Yeah. Didn't bark. Didn't run. Just sat down next to me like it already knew.
**Dog Profit:** Where were you?
**Scraps:** Alley off Third. It was raining. I had nowhere to be and I wasn't planning on going anywhere. You know what I mean.
*(Dog Profit knows what he means.)*
**Dog Profit:** How long did it stay?
**Scraps:** All night. I woke up and it was still there. Just looking at me like — like I owed it something. Like showing up was the least I could do.
**Dog Profit:** That ever happen to you before? Someone just... staying?
**Scraps:** No. Not once.
*(Silence. Dog Profit writes something down. Crosses it out. Writes it again.)*
**Dog Profit:** Me neither.

## INTERVIEW PART 2

**Scraps:** That's me in there.
**Dog Profit:** Yeah.
**Scraps:** And you.
**Dog Profit:** Yeah.
**Scraps:** The part about the shoes. If I was in your shoes I would be dead. That's real?
**Dog Profit:** I looked at myself the way I look at people who need saving and I thought — I wouldn't have done it. I would've kept walking.
**Scraps:** But you didn't.
**Dog Profit:** I didn't have a choice. The dog didn't give me one either, I guess. That's what you said, right? It just sat there like showing up was the least you could do.
**Scraps:** I was so angry when it stayed. I didn't want a witness.
**Dog Profit:** I know that feeling exactly.
**Scraps:** What changed for you?
**Dog Profit:** I stopped trying to black out everything that scared me. Started cutting the other thing instead.
**Scraps:** The demons.
**Dog Profit:** The demons. Yeah.
**Scraps:** Who showed you how?
**Dog Profit:** Someone who stayed.
