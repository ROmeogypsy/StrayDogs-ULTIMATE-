## 🎯 MEANING

### The Metaphor
- **Muzzle up** = Public restraint, strategic silence, don't draw attention
- **Teeth down** = Hidden danger, ready to bite, but not visibly aggressive
- **Pack mentality** = 66 Dogs moving as one
- **Bark vs. bite** = Loud threats (weak) vs. silent action (strong)

### Why It Matters
This is **The 10 Commandments** in song form before they were written:
- **Commandment #9:** Silence is gold (muzzle up)
- **Commandment #1:** Don't snitch (teeth down = don't bark to police)
- **Commandment #4:** Protect the set (pack mentality)
