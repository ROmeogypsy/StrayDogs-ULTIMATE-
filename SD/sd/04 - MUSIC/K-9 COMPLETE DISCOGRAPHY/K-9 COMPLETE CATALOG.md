## 🎯 SIGNATURE TECHNIQUES

### Chopper Flow
- Tech N9ne influence (Strange Music)
- 180+ BPM delivery
- Syllable stacking, breath control
- **Example tracks:** Chopper Drills, Muzzle Up

### Fibonacci Flow
- Mathematical structure (1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21)
- Syllable count follows golden ratio
- **Example track:** The Darkest Light

### Syllabic Embedding
- Learned from Angel post-album
- "Wine dykes → Whines rights" (Cyanide Shine)
- Multiple semantic layers in single phonetic sequence

### Backwards Text
- Learned from Zed
- Reversed phrases as cipher
- **Example track:** Nest Crease

### Midwest Chop
- Learned from Angel (The Thrizz Session)
- Bay Area hyphy style
- **Example tracks:** Thizz Tribute, Bay to the Grey
