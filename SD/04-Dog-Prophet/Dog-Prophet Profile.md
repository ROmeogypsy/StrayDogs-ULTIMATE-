## 🎤 Voice & Style
- **Energy:** Measured, oracular, weight in every syllable — the track that makes you sit still
- **Flow Tendencies:** Preacher cadence. Call-and-response structures. Verses feel like scripture.
- **Themes:** Prophecy and consequence, the street as sacred/profane space, judgment without court, legacy
- **Dialect:** Roadman filtered through old testament cadence. King James via North Ends.
- **Signature Move:** The commandment — a bar stated with such finality it ends the conversation
